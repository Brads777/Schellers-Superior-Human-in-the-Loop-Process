## Patent

**Patent Pending** — U.S. Provisional Patent Application

This repository contains technology that is the subject of a pending U.S. provisional patent application covering the methods, systems, and processes described herein, including but not limited to:

1. **Automated Multi-Source Research Pipeline for Evaluation Rubric Generation** — A system that orchestrates multiple AI research services (deep research synthesis, web search aggregation) and knowledge management platforms to automatically generate domain-specific evaluation criteria with graduated quality-level definitions.

2. **Research-Informed Human-AI Collaborative Rubric Construction** — A method for co-creating weighted, multi-level evaluation instruments through structured interview, where AI suggestions are grounded in synthesized research and the human refines criteria, weights, and level-specific definitions.

3. **Threshold-Gated Iterative Quality Assurance with Research-Cited Improvement Plans** — A system that evaluates outputs against bespoke rubrics, enforces a configurable quality threshold (default 96%), and generates prioritized improvement plans that cite specific research sources and reference exact level definitions to guide remediation.

4. **Distributable Evaluation Instruments as Executable Skills** — A method for packaging domain-specific evaluation rubrics as independently installable, versionable, and shareable skill artifacts within AI development environments.
